## What DIGIMON Is

DIGIMON is a composable GraphRAG system. An LLM controller chooses among
consolidated retrieval/build operators at runtime instead of following one fixed
pipeline.

**Current thesis worth testing:** the adaptive control layer can outperform a
fixed retrieval strategy on multi-hop QA.

**Current benchmark frontier:** MuSiQue, with `LLM_EM` as the maintained
development metric and 50-question runs as the decision-grade gate.

**Key files**
- `Core/MCP/tool_consolidation.py` — consolidated tool surface
- `digimon_mcp_stdio_server.py` — query-rewrite, submit, todo, and MCP behavior
- `prompts/agent_benchmark_consolidated.yaml` — active benchmark prompt (`Prompt v3.6`)
- `eval/run_agent_benchmark.py` — benchmark runner
- `CURRENT_STATUS.md` — maintained status snapshot
- `docs/plans/28_truthful_overnight_stabilization_and_contract_repair.md` — active truth/repair plan

   and whether a later bridge candidate replaces the already-grounded entity

   `exact_anchor_drift | bridge_displacement | IEE | answer_kind | year_disambiguation | other`
